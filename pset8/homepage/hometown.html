Hometown

As stated on my about me page, I am originally from Wigan, a large town in the metropolitan area of Manchester.
Wigan is an old town with history dating back to the Roman occupation. In fact several Roman buildings have been uncovered while building has been done in the town centre
It has a long history of mining and has a national repuation as 'pie eaters.' This originally came from miners having to go back to work after a strike and therefore had to eat 'humble pie'
This reputation has evolved over the years to just centre around eating pies and a world championship pie eating contest is held in a Wigan pub every year.
